DjangoCon US 2016 in Philly

We are pleased to announce that the DjangoCon US 2016 conference will take place in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania from July 17-22, 2016 at The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania:



July 17: Tutorials

July 18-20: Talks

July 21-22: Sprints



We have a lot of amazing things planned that we’ll announce over the next few weeks. Until then, here’s what you can do to help!

Mark your calendars and help us spread the word! Start thinking about topics for when our Call for Proposals is announced.

Watch this space, @DjangoCon on Twitter, or follow the discussions on the djangocon-organizers list. Sponsor the event! Want to help by joining our organizers? Start working on those proposals, and we hope we'll see you at DjangoCon US!
